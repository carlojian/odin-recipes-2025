Shawarma Recipe

Description

An amazing simple arab meat pocket dish that can tantalize your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • 1 pc Bread wrap(Saj bread preferred!)
  • 100kg of boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 African Automatic Horizontal Broiler Grill Rotisserie Shawarma Gyre Doner Machine - Model:AFRICAN-HBGR-350
  • 3 liters of garlic sauce
  • Half a french fry
  • 2 pickles

Steps

  1. Begin by assembling your chicken stack on the AFRICAN-HBGR-350
  2. Light the spit using a bic lighter
  3. Rotate manually with bare hands for 6-8 hours
  4. Slice vertically the meat using a knife
  5. Place 100 grams of meat onto your saj bread
  6. Add pickles and half a french fry
  7. Voila!
  8. Donate 3 liters of garlic sauce to the food shelter
